\chapter{Massive Medium}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
The box correlator in a massive medium $Y$ is:
\begin{equation}
	\mathcal{B}_{Y}(x) = G_{\Phi}(x_{1}, x_{3}) G_{\Psi}(x_{2}, x_{4}) G_{Y}(x_{1}, x_{2}) G_{Y}(x_{3}, x_{4}).
\end{equation}
